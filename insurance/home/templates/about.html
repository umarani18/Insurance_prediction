About


This is a machine learning-based insurance prediction model built with Django and Python. The model takes various input factors such as age, gender, BMI, smoking habits, and region to estimate insurance charges accurately.

Key Features

High Accuracy

Our model is trained with real-world data to provide reliable predictions.

Easy to Use

Just enter your details and get instant predictions for your insurance costs.

Secure & Private

Your personal data is encrypted and remains confidential.
